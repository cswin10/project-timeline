# Technical FAQ - For Client Questions

## "How does the AI actually work?"

**Simple Answer:**
"It uses GPT-4 Vision - the same AI technology that powers ChatGPT, but specialized for analyzing images. It 'reads' architectural drawings like an experienced estimator would, identifying rooms, measuring spaces, and noting complexity. Then it applies rules based on YOUR company's historical data to generate accurate timelines."

**If they want more detail:**
"The AI is trained on millions of images, so it can recognize architectural drawings, floor plans, and construction details. When you upload a drawing, it extracts room counts, estimates square footage, identifies features like bathrooms and kitchens, and notes structural complexity. Then it uses mathematical rules we configure based on your historical project data to calculate realistic timelines."
